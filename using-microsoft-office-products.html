Microsoft Products

This article focuses on how to use the selected Microsoft products as tools to enable teachers to compile their work.
Here the assumption is that the following products have been installed on your laptop or desktop machine.
However, if you don't have Office 365 apps installed on your device,
you can visit this website which gives step by step instructions on how to install Office.
Additionally, you will find different sections depending on the version that you would love to install. Common products on Office 365 applications for teachers are:

  • Microsoft Word
  • Microsot Powerpoint
  • Microsoft Excel

Word 2016

This application serves as a tool for type writing documents. There are several kinds of documents that one may create. Examples are; question papers, assignments, book-length projects, letters, essays, flyers, invitation cards, curriculum vitaes (CVs) and even drafting achievement certificates for learners. The list is exhaustive and endless but this one tool of putting your ideas, thoughts and projects in writing, is very powerful. The following video gives a very introductory usage of Word 2016.

Additional resources on Office 2016 can be found here where you can learn free of charge. This is a very insightful and inclusive website that targets different audience and technological resources. It is also a go-to wesite if require advancement on a this topic and others.

Powerpoint 2016

This product is used mainly to prepare presentation slides for many occasions such as classrooom lessons and pitching business ideas. It can also be used to
share teaching and learning milestones of pupils with your colleaques at boardrooms or workshops. When attending teachers conferences, this is the main
powerful tool for preparing your slides for an oral talk and a poster for conveying your message in a flexible and interactive manner. In this video, a complete
tutorial gives a guide on how to use Powerpoint 2016 is given. The tutorial is quite long but for a shorter version, you are welcome to follow this
link.

Excel 2016

Excel is a good tool for managing anything that has got to do with numbers. Whether you are recording learners marks, drafting a school's trip budget,
or even your own monthly income and expenditure budget, this is the go-to application that proves that indeed numbers-make-sense to anyone needless of having
an advanced mathematical background. For a speedy and short introduction into developing and managing Excel spreadsheets,
this link will give you a good start. However, if you need a deep dive into this topic, please watch the next video at your own pace.
